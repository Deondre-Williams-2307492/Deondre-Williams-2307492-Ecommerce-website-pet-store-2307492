About Critterly

Founded in 1993 in the heart of East Kingston, Jamaica, Crittley Pet Store has been a beloved destination for pet lovers for over 30 years. What began as a small, family-run shop has grown into a vibrant hub for animal enthusiasts, offering a wide range of products and personalized care that keeps tails wagging and whiskers twitching. At Crittley, we believe pets are more than companions — they're family. That's why we stock only the best:

  • Healthy, natural pet foods for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, and small animals
  • Aquatic essentials including tanks, filters, and a variety of fish
  • Pet accessories like collars, toys, grooming tools, and bedding
  • Live animals including small mammals, birds, and reptiles — all cared for by our passionate team

Our staff is made up of dedicated animal lovers who take pride in helping you find the perfect products and companions. Whether you're a first-time pet parent or a seasoned caretaker, we,re here to guide you with expert advice and heartfelt service. We're proud to be a locally owned business that brings joy, knowledge, and community spirit to every customer who walks through our doors. Thank you for supporting Crittley — where every pawprint matters.
